About me

Yulia was born in Kiev, Ukraine in 1989. Before turning one her family move to Bogota, Colombia to live with her father's family. In Colombia her father and mother got a divorce. Then before turning six her mother and two sisters moved back to Ukraine to live with her grandma. Her mother remarried, fire years later, to an American. Thus moving the family to Arizona.

In United States, Yulia went to a college prep school/preforming arts school grades 6th to 12th. After graduating high school, Yulia enrolled at ASU. Five years later she graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Manufacturing Engineering and Technology. Then she found herself getting a job as a Digital Equipment Technician at Shutterfly.
